About Us

Mathew is a dedicated and experienced physiotherapist with over 12 years of diverse experience since completing his Bachelor of Physiotherapy with internship training in 2008. He has specialised in key areas, including neurological rehabilitation, orthopaedics, and ICU rehabilitation within cardiorespiratory units. His career has spanned renowned hospitals, private practices, aged care, and community health, demonstrating his versatility and commitment to patient care.

In 2023, Mathew further expanded his expertise by obtaining a Graduate Certificate in Health Administration from Monash University, adding valuable administrative skills to support his clinical practice. He is passionate about empowering his patients to reach their health goals, developing personalised treatment plans tailored to each individual’s unique needs. Mathew’s positive approach ensures he takes the time to fully address each condition, fostering long-term recovery and meaningful outcomes.

Mathew places a strong emphasis on patient education, believing that informed individuals achieve better results and lead fuller lives through effective physiotherapy. This dedication not only aids recovery but also helps prevent future injuries, enhancing his patients’ overall quality of life. Mathew’s fluency in English, Malayalam, Tamil, and Hindi allows him to connect with a diverse range of clients, enriching his ability to understand and meet their needs.
